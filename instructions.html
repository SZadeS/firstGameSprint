Instructions

Two icons will appear for 12 seconds when the game begins: footsteps and skulls

Memorise the exact location of the steps. Then, starting from the bottom row, working upwards, recreate the location of the steps

If you have already chosen a box but wish to remove that choice, just click it again! However, one click before your last, this option will not be available!
